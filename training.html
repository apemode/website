Team Training

Are you looking to bring your sports team to the next level? Antonios Assaad, expert in the field of fine tuning athletic performance on a individual and team setting has been known internationally for the performance of his teams.

We offer training programs year round:

  • Off-season Training
  • In-season Training
  • Pre-season Training

What to expect:

  • Performance Assessment to determine weaknesses and areas of improvement for each player
  • Strength and Conditioning the most important part of preparing young athletes to endure a hard workload of a season
  • Warm up and Individual Mobility Exercises educating everyone on how to properly warm up and cool down for practices and matches.
  • Endurance, Speed and Agility exercises targeting be position specific distances and intensity levels.
    Eg. Goalies will train for more Explosive movements. Wingers & Outside Backs will focus more on box to box interval running sprints etc.
