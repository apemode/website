Performance Assessments

Every player is different. We take each player through tests to determine weaknesses and areas of improvement. Test like the overhead squat assessment are used to measure overall musculoskeletal function. Imbalances are recorded and help us plan future training.

Strength and Conditioning

The most important part of preparing young athletes to endure a hard workload of a season. Increasing potential performance in the off-season has many benefits and is the foundation of personal athletic growth. It’s important to maintain athletic prowess, because it’s much easier to stay in shape than get back into shape!

Warm Up and Individual Mobility Exercises

Educating everyone on how to properly warm up and cool down for practices and matches. This is critical for preventing injuries and ensuring athletes are performing at their best.

Targeted Endurance, Speed and Agility Training

Each position should be trained differently. We do not train a striker to function exactly like a central defender. Strikers need reaction exercises, explosive movements, jumping and switching directions.
