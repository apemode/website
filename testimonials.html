Testimonials

Our youth soccer team has been training with Antonios for the past month. His training sessions are challenging, fun, and most importantly soccer related. Our sessions consist of functional movements that soccer players complete during a match (sprints with changes of direction, jumps, agility, etc.). We also work on improving core, leg, and upper body strength as well as flexibility/mobility and injury prevention.

Antonios is constantly pushing and motivating the players to surpass their perceived limitations. The boys have noticeably gained a lot of self-confidence and increased their team spirit and camaraderie in the past month.

His experience as a professional player helps players understand all aspects of an athlete's lifestyle such as nutrition, rest, and mentality.

The team is always looking forward to each session.
